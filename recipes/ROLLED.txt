Blend grated cheese with seasonings and mayonnaise. Use Merita Thin-Sliced Bread for rolled sandwiches. Spread thinly with soft butter or margarine and cheese filling_. Trim crusts; roll each slice into a cylinder. Parsley or watercress may be tucked in ends. Wrap in waxed paper; chill 30 minutes or longer in refrigerator. Makes about 14 sandwiches.
