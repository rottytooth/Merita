CRAB CANAPES 

1	cup flaked (3 oz.) crabmeat 
2	hard-cooked eggs 
1	tsp. lemon juice 
3	tbsp. mayonnaise 
½	tsp. Worcestershire Sauce
	Dash of tabasco sauce 
	Salt and pepper 
	Ripe olives or capers 
	Cream cheese 
	Merita Thin-Sliced Bread

Mix crabmeat with the chopped egg and seasonings. Cut Bread with diamond cutter; toast and spread with soft butter and crab mixture. Decorate with cream cheese (blend ½ cup cream cheese with 2 tbsp. mayonnaise and ¾ tsp. Worcestershire Sauce); put through pastry bag. Garnish. (24 canapes)
