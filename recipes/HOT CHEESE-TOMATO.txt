HOT CHEESE-TOMATO

¼	lb. sliced American Cheese 
	Merita Thin-Sliced Bread
2	ripe tomatoes 
	Stuffed olives 

Mustard-Butter: Blend ¾ cup soft butter or margarine with 1 tablespoon prepared mustard or ½ teaspoon dry mustard. Spread untoasted side of Merita Bread with soft butter or mayonnaise, place slice cheese on each. Trim crusts; cut in squares, arrange thin wedges of tomato (or tomato slice in quarters) on cheese; broil until cheese melts. Garnish center. Serve hot. (20 canapes)
