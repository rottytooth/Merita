DEVILED TONGUE

1	cup ground cooked smoked beef tongue 
1	tbsp. grated horse radish 
1	tbsp. pickle relish
1	tsp. Worcestershire Sauce 
2	tbsp. salad dressing 
	Merita Wheat Bread 
	Pimiento--Olives 

Combine tongue, horseradish, pickle relish and Worcestershire Sauce; add salad dressing for spreading consistency. Trim crusts of Merita Wheat Bread; cut in strips and toast lightly on one side. Spread untoasted side with soft butter or margarine and tongue mixture. Garnish with lengthwise cuts of stuffed olives. (24 canapes.)
